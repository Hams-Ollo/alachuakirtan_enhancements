Festival of the Holy Name

Mission Statement

We aim to unite our diverse Alachua Hare Krishna community through focused chanting of the Hare Krishna maha-mantra. We relish the sweetness of connection with Sri Krishna and His Holy Name in an egalitarian, family-friendly atmosphere where every person is considered and included. By sitting together on an equal level, facing the Deities, we attempt to fully focus on the Lord’s form and the pure essence of the Holy Name. We honor this gift of kirtan, and aspire to create an atmosphere of joyful camaraderie in experiencing kirtan, performing seva (service), and honoring prasada together.

 

 

Singer Guidelines

 

Thank you for agreeing to sing in service of Sri Krishna and the Holy Name at the Festival of the Holy Name. We are grateful for your participation and help in this yajña, serving the Holy Name together.

We ask you to please help serve the intention of the festival, read our ethos document, and come in the mood of service to the festival and Holy Name.

 

Please only chant the Hare Krishna maha-mantra. Pranam mantras to Srila Prabhupada (and invocation mantras) are chanted at the beginning of each day. We request you to also refrain from sharing inspirational words or quotes before or after your kirtan. Chanting only the Hare Krishna mantra helps focus our attention on the Holy Name and keeps this kirtan service connected to the simple and pure essence of this recommended yuga dharma.

 

Please sing in your scheduled time slot and refrain from giving your time away to anyone else. If you would like to withdraw from your singing service, please contact us in advance. We asked you to sing because we appreciate your unique style and flavor and how that connects with and inspires the devotees.

 

Please end your kirtan at the scheduled time. This ensures that the person singing after you can start on time and that the schedule isn’t delayed.

 

Our sound service is provided on a best-effort basis and our simplicity is intentional. The kirtan setup for this festival has the lead singer and musicians seated in a semicircle facing Sri Sri Nitai-Gaurachandra to best focus on the Holy Name and Their Lordships.

 

Please arrange for your support musicians or contact us if you need assistance. Please arrange for a mridanga player or any other musicians during your time slot.

 

If there’s a special instrument you would like to connect to the sound system, please let us know prior to the start of the festival. It’s difficult for us to accommodate last-minute connections to the sound system in the middle of the festival.

 

Please be at the kirtan tent at least 15 minutes before your scheduled time so you can be oriented and prepared to sing during your time slot. Or ideally, please try to participate in the kirtans during the entire day.

 

Thank you so much for your service! We appreciate you facilitating the devotees in this important way. We hope you are inspired to participate in the full festival and other kirtans. Please contact us if you have any questions or special requests.
